About me

Hey! My name is Anushka.
I am a software developer and mindful meditator, I've found that both spirituality and technology are crucial in creating innovative solutions. I am passionate about bridging the gap between these seemingly opposite fields and helping others discover the benefits of mindfulness and meditation in their professional lives.

My interest in technology has always been driven by a curiosity about how things work and a desire to create something that makes a real impact on people's lives. As a software developer, I'm fascinated by the endless possibilities that technology provides, and I'm constantly seeking out new technologies and tools to help me build better and more innovative solutions. What excites me most about technology is its potential to solve complex problems and make a positive difference in the world. I'm always eager to learn and grow, and I believe that technology is one of the most exciting and dynamic fields to be a part of.

In my personal life, I've been practicing meditation for four years and have found that it has helped me become a more focused and creative problem solver. I believe that by bringing a mindful approach to anything we do, we can create more impactful and meaningful solutions.

My mission is to inspire others to explore the intersection of spirituality and technology and to help them discover the benefits of incorporating mindfulness practices into their work.

If you share these values and are interested in learning more, let's connect!
